About

A seasoned aviation expert who turns standards into working systems

I am a civil aviation professional with extensive experience developing and implementing complex safety and management systems. Over a career that progressed from trainee to Head of Division, I have established new organizational units, developed national aviation regulations and roadmaps, and represented the aviation administration on international platforms.

Since 2022 I serve as the National Coordinator for cooperation with ICAO (USOAP CMA and USAP CMA), aligning national regulations with ICAO Annexes through the Electronic Filing of Differences (EFOD) system. I have led the rollout of Safety Management Systems (SMS), Performance-Based Navigation (PBN) and Runway Safety Programmes, and delivered advisory work internationally — including engagements in Iraq and Saudi Arabia.

My approach combines a strong analytical foundation — a degree in Applied Mathematics and Power BI analytics — with hands-on regulatory and operational experience, so recommendations are both rigorous and implementable.
